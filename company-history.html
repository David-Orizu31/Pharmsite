Randhawa Pharma Pharmaceutical Ltd.

Randhawa Pharm Ltd specializes in the importation and distribution of medical and hospital consumables. Our commitment and drive has propelled our growth from a single marketing company to a world class Pharmaceutical company. From working with small businesses to big conglomerates, Randhawa Pharma Pharmaceutical Ltd works round the clock to bring our clients imaginations to life and move them forward through innovation. We aim to meet the needs of the healthcare sector by providing easy access to internationally certified quality products that assist in the rendering of healthcare services. Also, we provide a wide range of services to our customers and distributors such as delivery and processing of orders over the telephone or internet.
